# Disclaimer

This repository is for security research, documentation, and educational purposes.

The material here documents suspected malware indicators in firmware associated with the INOI A75 Elegance device. The findings are based on static analysis and reproducible artifacts collected during research.

## No Overstatement

The repository should distinguish clearly between:

- confirmed artifacts
- technical interpretation
- open questions
- unverified assumptions

A detection or suspicious artifact should not be described as proof of intent by a manufacturer, distributor, reseller, or third party unless that claim is supported by evidence.

## Malware Safety

Firmware samples, extracted payloads, DEX files, native libraries, scripts, or binary artifacts related to this research may be malicious.

Do not execute samples on a personal computer or daily-use phone. Use an isolated lab environment, read-only mounts, snapshots, and network isolation.
